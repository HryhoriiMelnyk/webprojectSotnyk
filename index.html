About us

The Sky Mantis all weather long endurance UAV is now widely deployed with UK and international Police Forces, Fire Services, Energy inspection, Search & Rescue organisations and UK MoD (Ministry of Defence) customers. Evolve Dynamics is continuously developing new payloads and platforms - eVTOL, nano, micro, EW to name a few. Get in touch to discuss your requirements
